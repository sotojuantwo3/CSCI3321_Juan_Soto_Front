Chicken and Shrimp Alfredo

Ingredients












Directions

  1. Boil water in a pot big enough for the pasta. Once boiling, add in the pasta with the italian spice and garlic salt. Stir the pasta every 5-10 minutes until soft (or preferred texture).
  2. While the pasta boils, add the butter to a separate pan. Once butter has melted, add the vegetable to the pan and cook for 4 minutes.
  3. Add and cook the chicken and shrimp in cajun seasoning until ready.
  4. Once the pasta is done, save a coffee mug size of the pasta water for flavor and strain the pasta. Put it back into the pot with the pasta water and add the Alfredo sauce.
  5. Place pot back over the stove (used to boil the pasta), reduce the stove temperature to LOW, and stir the pasta in the sauce.
  6. Once the pasta has been stirred with the sauce, add the chicken and shrimp to the pasta and mix again.
  7. Let it set for 3 minutes and then it is ready to serve.
